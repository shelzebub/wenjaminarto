Hello! My name is Wenny and I'm a 2019 Sheridan Animation graduate interested in animation, layout, and design. I have worked on prop designs for a 3D Netflix series as well as two 2D-animated projects.

My first collaborative film project is "Borborygmus" which consisted of about 10 other members. My role was production manager and I was involved in character/prop design, animation, layout as well as sound editing and compositing.

During the Summer of 2018, I interned at Kickstart Entertainment in Vancouver, BC as an Animation Artist and Prop Designer for a 2D and a 3D production. I was also nominated as one of the top co-op students of the year for the Faculty of Animation, Arts and Design. ​ Currently, I am in the process of completing my thesis film, "Feed Me"!
